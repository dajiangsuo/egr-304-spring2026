***Team Assignment***

## Objectives

To create a "pitch video" introducing yourselves, talking briefly about your project, and demonstrating your project.

## Resources

-   [Creating a local recording with Zoom](https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/201362473-Local-Recording)

-   [Creating a cloud recording with Zoom](https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/203741855-Cloud-recording)

-   Canvas Discussion Board

## Assignment

1.  Create a 2 - 3 minute video pitching your project to the general public. Your video should include:

    1. Your names and that you are engineering students in The Polytechnic School at ASU

    1. A brief description of your project

    1. A brief demonstration of your project
        > 
        > Your video should be energetic and get people excited about your project. It **should not** be a technical walkthrough of your project. All team members must appear in the video. With your permission, winning teams will be asked if their videos can be shared on the i.d.e.a. Museum's social media *(optional)*
        > 
        > No fancy video equipment or editing is required (Zoom recordings / smartphone / laptop camera OK)

2.  Upload the video to YouTube (unlisted privacy) or to your Google Drive


### Canvas Submission

Submit a link (YouTube or Google Drive) to your video to this assignment on Canvas by the deadline in Canvas. It is your responsibility to ensure that your submission to Canvas was successful. Late Canvas submissions will be graded per the policy in the syllabus. No credit will be awarded for assignments not submitted to Canvas.
