***Team Assignment***

## Objectives

To discuss times that your team can meet outside of class

## Resources

-   [Contact Information and Weekly Schedule Template](https://www.dropbox.com/s/ucr49ujowc9rkb2/Contact%20Information%20and%20Weekly%20Schedule.xlsx?dl=0)

## Procedure

1.  Fill out the linked [template](https://www.dropbox.com/s/ucr49ujowc9rkb2/Contact%20Information%20and%20Weekly%20Schedule.xlsx?dl=0). Put abbreviations next to hours in the week you ***are certain*** you will be generally available ***throughout** the semester*. Consider your other hard constraints in a typical week, such as other classes, work, transit, meals, or family commitments.

2.  Identify at least ***four*** hours shared by the team ***outside of class time.***

3.  Enter preferred contact information for all team members.

4.  Decide as a team what technological platform(s) (e.g., group text, canvas, slack, discord, email) you will use to communicate with your entire team at once

## Submission

Submit your completed team schedule in PDF format to the Team Contact Information and Schedule assignment on [Canvas](https://canvas.asu.edu) by the deadline stated in the calendar. *Do not submit links to online documents.*
