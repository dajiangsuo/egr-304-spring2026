Each team will create and maintain a virtual folder structure containing the latest versions of the documents listed below. You must use a plain Google Drive folder, not a Google "Shared Drive". This specification and the naming convention below is intentional to support our administration of the course. Follow it exactly as specified.

## Submission Instructions

In addition to sharing with the teaching team as requested above, submit a link to your team's Google Drive Folder on [Canvas](http://canvas.asu.edu) by the deadline in Canvas. It is your responsibility to ensure that the submission to Canvas was successful. Late Canvas submissions will be graded per the policy in the syllabus. No credit will be awarded for assignments not submitted to Canvas.
