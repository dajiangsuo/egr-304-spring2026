## Instructions

1.  You will receive a link from CATME to your ASU email address (```ASURITE@asu.edu```). Check your spam folder if you do not receive it.

2.  Fill out the survey online.

3.  Await your team evaluations.

## Canvas Submission

None required

## Grading

Each student will receive an "Adjustment Factor w/o Self" rating between 0.0 and 1.05 based on how their team members evaluated their performance. The final score for this assignment will be calculated as follows:

$\left( a,p \right) = p \times \frac{a}{1.05}$

where $a$ is your "Adj Factor w/o Self" and $p$ is the maximum points possible for this assignment.
