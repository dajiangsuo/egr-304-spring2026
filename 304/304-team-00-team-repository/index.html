    Instructions

    Team Folder Structure

    Each team will create and maintain a virtual folder structure containing the latest versions of the documents listed below. You must use a plain Google Drive folder, not a Google "Shared Drive". This specification and the naming convention below is intentional to support our administration of the course. Follow it exactly as specified.

    Do not create and share a Google "Shared Drive" with us.

    1. Go to Google Drive and create a folder named "EGR304 F24 Team 123 - Project Name", where "123" is your three-digit team number and "Project Name" is a 1-3 word description of your project.

      Examples:

      • EGR304 F24 Team 105 - Automated Suspenders
      • EGR304 F24 Team 207 - Monkey Fitbit

    2. Share your folder with the following people (as editors):

    Submission Instructions

    This work will be used in multiple ways. It will be reviewed for feedback in class on the date given in Canvas, so please be prepared to discuss it with a member of the teaching team.

    Canvas Submission

    Google Drive Folder

    In addition to sharing with the teaching team as requested above, submit a link to your team's Google Drive Folder on Canvas by the deadline in Canvas. It is your responsibility to ensure that the submission to Canvas was successful. Late Canvas submissions will be graded per the policy in the syllabus. No credit will be awarded for assignments not submitted to Canvas.

    Subsequent Uses of this document

    Classtime

    This document should be made available during classtime for review by the teaching team on the due date. We will provide feedback, which will be your responsibility to integrate and address in subsequent submissions.

    Report

    This assignment establishes the structure for storing all the docuements included in your team's report and must be maintained throughout the semester. It will be evaluated in upcoming project checkpoints.

    Checkpoints

    The folder will be reviewed for all three checkpoints.
